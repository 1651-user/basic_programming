LoRaWAN sensors ensure vaccines remain at safe temperatures during transport.

LoRaWAN enables early detection of leaks in city water networks.

Food Safety

Temperature monitoring ensures that food stays within safe quality standards.

Smart Waste Bins

Bin-level alerts help optimize waste collection routes.

Smart Bikes

Bike trackers locate bikes in remote or dense urban areas.

Airport Tracking

GPS-free tracking helps monitor vehicles, staff, and luggage.

Efficient Workspaces

Monitoring room occupancy, temperature, energy use, and parking availability.

Cattle Health

Sensors detect cattle diseases early and predict calf delivery times.

LoRa in Space

Satellites provide global LoRaWAN coverage even in remote regions.
